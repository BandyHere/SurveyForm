Lil Uzi Vert

The Purple Emperor

Timeline of Uzi's life:

  • 1994 - Symere Woods was born in North Philadelphia, Francisville
  • 2007 - Symere Woods would grow up listening to artist like Mike Jones, The Yin Yang Twins, Wiz Khalifa, and Meek Mill. They also began listeing to artist Marilyn Manson, Paramore, Smash Mouth, The Rocket Summer, Simian, My Chemical Romance, and The All-American Rejects, all belived to be influnce for his style of music.
  • 2011 - Woods would go on to be part of a rap group called Steaktown, which would break up when he turned 17.
  • 2014 - Woods, from this point on would go by the stage name Lil Uzi Vert in refrence to his style of rapping,which was fast like a machinegun. In January of the same year, Uzi would drop his first project, an EP titled Purple Thoughtz Vol. 1.
  • 2014 - Purple Thoughtz Vol. 1. would go on to catch the attetion of the producer Don Cannon, who would later sign Uzi to the imprint The Academy, and produce Uzi's first mixtape The Real Uzi. 1.(Released August 15, 2014)
  • 2015 - Uzi officaly signed to Don Cannon's record label Alantic Records and would drop his second mixtape Luv is Rage. 1 on October 30th, 2015
  • 2016 - 2016 is seen by many as the breakthrough of Uzi's career. In this year Uzi would drop two hit single "Money Longer" and "You Was Right" both appearing on Hot Billboard's top 100 chart. He would also go on to appear in XXL Magazine's freshman class and drop his fourth mixtape The Perfect Luv Tape
  • 2017- Uzi's career continued it's momentum in 2017 with the realease of his first studio album Luv is Rage 2 largest song ever "XO Tour Lif3" and other classics like "Woke Up Like This", "Bad and Boujie" and many more.
  • 2018 - Uzi would tease the realease of his second album Eternal Atake with images based of the cult Heavens Gate on twitter. After being delayed due to legal troubles Uzi would drop hit songs like "New Patek" to hold fans off.
  • 2019 - It was this year Uzi announced his transfer from Alantic records to Roc Nation and promted two new singles "Sanguine Paradise" and "That's a Rack" expexted to appear on the highly awaited Eternal Atake.
  • 2020 - Eternal Atake and its Delux verison were released on March 6, 2020 and April 24, 2020 respectively, launching Uzi back into the public eye.
  • 2021 - Uzi would begin to tease his new album The Pink Tape
  • 2022 - It was teased July 12, 2022, they would release an EP titled Red & White prior to the release of their third studio album Pink Tape. [105] Red & White was released ten days later, with Uzi releasing a number of highly anticipated tracks exclusively on SoundCloud in the lead-up to the EP's release.

    • Marilyn Manson is referred to be their "biggest inspiration" by Lil Uzi Vert. They also like the band Paramore, claiming frontwoman Hayley Williams as an inspiration. Lil Uzi Vert mentioned the Ying Yang Twins, ASAP Rocky, Pharrell Williams, Kanye West, Simple Plan, Young Thug, and Wiz Khalifa in an interview with Complex as influences.
